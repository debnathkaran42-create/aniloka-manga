Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information AniLoka ("we", "us", "the platform") collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. AniLoka is a manga reading platform, and this policy applies to [yourdomain.com] and the AniLoka app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: username, email address, and a securely hashed password (we never store your actual password).
  • Reading activity: bookmarks, reading history, and progress, so you can pick up where you left off.
  • Payment information: when you pay via UPI, we store the transaction amount, the UPI Transaction ID (UTR) you submit, and the payment status. We do not collect or store your UPI PIN, bank account number, or card details — those never touch AniLoka, they stay inside your UPI app and bank.
  • Technical information: basic device/browser information and, where advertising is enabled, information collected by our ad partner (see Section 4).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account and reading history.
  • To verify UPI payments manually and activate Premium or unlock purchased chapters.
  • To send you notifications about your account, purchases, or new chapters.
  • To keep the platform secure (e.g. rate-limiting login attempts).

3. Where Your Data Lives

Depending on how this deployment of AniLoka is configured, your data is stored either locally in your browser (local-only mode) or in AniLoka's own database on our server (when a backend is connected). We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Google AdSense (if ads are enabled) — may use cookies to show relevant ads. See Google's own Privacy Policy.
  • Razorpay (if enabled as a payment option) — processes card/automatic payments; AniLoka never sees your full card details.

5. Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us (see Contact Us). You can also delete your account data by clearing your browser's local storage if you're using local-only mode.

6. Children's Privacy

AniLoka is not directed at children under 13. If we learn a child under 13 has provided us personal information, we will delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of AniLoka after changes means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at [privacy@yourdomain.com].
