Content Licensing Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy covers how licensed or partnered manga content is handled on AniLoka, separate from creator self-published work (see the Creator Agreement for that).

1. Licensed Content

Where AniLoka has a direct licensing agreement or partnership with a publisher/rightsholder, that content is published under the specific terms of that agreement, which may include exclusivity windows, regional restrictions, or simulpub schedules.

2. Attribution

Licensed titles display the original author, artist, and publisher wherever that information is available, matching how it's credited in the source release.

3. Regional Restrictions

Some licensed content may only be available to readers in specific regions, per the terms of the licensing agreement. [Specify regions if applicable].

4. Anti-Piracy Measures

Licensed content is protected the same way as all AniLoka content — watermarking, screenshot/recording deterrents, and paid-chapter access controls — see our Terms for the acceptable use rules that apply.

5. Redistribution

No content on AniLoka, licensed or creator-published, may be downloaded in bulk, re-hosted, or redistributed elsewhere without written permission.

6. Partnership Inquiries

Publishers interested in a licensing partnership with AniLoka can reach out via Contact Us.
