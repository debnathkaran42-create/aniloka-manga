Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy explains how refunds work for Premium plans and individual chapter purchases made through AniLoka.

Because payments are verified manually against a bank statement, nothing is charged or unlocked until an admin approves your UTR. If your payment is rejected, no access was ever granted and no charge was processed by AniLoka — any money that left your account was between you and your bank/UPI app, and should be automatically reversed by your bank within a few days if the transaction failed on their end.

1. Digital Goods

Premium access and unlocked chapters are digital goods delivered instantly upon approval. As a general rule, purchases are non-refundable once approved and access has been granted.

2. When We Will Refund You

  • You were charged twice for the same purchase (duplicate UTR submitted/approved by mistake).
  • You were charged an incorrect amount due to a pricing error on our end.
  • A technical error on AniLoka's side prevented you from accessing what you paid for.

3. How to Request a Refund

Contact us at [support@yourdomain.com] within 7 days of the purchase with your UTR, the amount, and a description of the issue. We aim to respond within 3–5 business days.

4. Refund Method

Approved refunds are sent back to the original UPI ID used for payment. UPI refunds typically take 5–7 business days to reflect, depending on your bank.

5. Subscription Cancellations

You can cancel your Premium plan anytime from your Profile — this stops future renewals but does not refund the current billing period already paid for.
