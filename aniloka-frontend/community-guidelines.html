Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 2026

AniLoka is meant to be a welcoming place for manga readers. These guidelines apply to reviews, comments, and any other place you interact with other users.

1. Be Respectful

No harassment, hate speech, threats, or personal attacks against other users, creators, or staff.

2. No Spam

Don't post repetitive, irrelevant, or promotional content in reviews or comments.

3. Spoilers

Be considerate — avoid major spoilers in review titles or the first line of a review where other readers can't avoid them.

4. Age-Appropriate Content

Keep reviews and comments appropriate for a general audience. Explicit or graphic content in comments is not allowed, regardless of the manga's own content rating.

5. No Piracy Talk

Don't use reviews/comments to link to or promote pirated copies of manga, on AniLoka or elsewhere.

6. Reporting

If you see content that violates these guidelines, please report it or contact us at [support@yourdomain.com].

7. Consequences

Violations may result in content removal, a warning, temporary suspension, or permanent account termination for serious or repeated violations.
