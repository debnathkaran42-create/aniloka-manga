Creator Partnership Agreement

Last updated: July 2026
Download as PDF
This is a real bilateral contract template, meant to be filled in and signed by both AniLoka and a Creator — different from the general Creator Agreement (which covers standard terms for anyone uploading to AniLoka). Have a lawyer review this before you send it to a real creator — money and exclusivity are involved, and requirements vary by state/country.

This Creator Partnership Agreement ("Agreement") is made between:

AniLoka, represented by [Representative Name & Title] ("AniLoka", "Platform", "we"/"us"), and

[Creator Full Legal Name], also publishing as [Pen Name / Studio Name, if any] ("Creator", "you"), for the manga title(s) [Manga Title(s)] ("the Work"),

effective as of [Effective Date] ("Effective Date").

1. Grant of Rights

Creator grants AniLoka a license to host, reproduce, display, distribute, and monetize the Work on the AniLoka platform (web and any AniLoka mobile apps), including through Premium subscriptions and individual paid-chapter unlocks, for the Term of this Agreement. Creator retains full ownership and copyright of the Work at all times — this Agreement is a license, not a transfer of ownership.

2. Exclusivity

During the Term of this Agreement, Creator agrees to publish the Work exclusively on AniLoka and will not publish the same chapters, in the same language, on any other manga/webtoon platform, website, or app without AniLoka's prior written consent. This does not restrict Creator from:

  • Posting promotional excerpts, cover art, or announcements on personal social media;
  • Continuing to sell existing print/physical editions through other channels, unless separately agreed;
  • Publishing entirely different, unrelated works elsewhere.

3. Revenue Share

Creator earns a share of net revenue AniLoka collects that is directly attributable to the Work (Premium chapter unlocks, and a proportional share of Premium subscription revenue where applicable), according to the tier assigned below:

TierCreator ShareAniLoka ShareApplies When
Standard70%30%Default tier for all creator partnerships
Select90%10%Creators specifically designated "Select" in writing by AniLoka — see Schedule A

This Agreement applies the [Standard / Select] tier to Creator, effective from the date above. Criteria for Select-tier eligibility (e.g. exclusivity track record, audience size, invited partnership) are set by AniLoka and documented in [Schedule A / a separate written addendum], referenced here by attachment. Tier changes require a signed written amendment or addendum — not a unilateral change by either party.

4. Payment Terms

Revenue is calculated and paid out on a [monthly / other] basis, provided accrued earnings meet a minimum payout threshold of [₹ amount]. Payments are made to [Creator's UPI ID / bank account details, on file separately]. AniLoka will provide Creator a statement showing unlocks, subscriber-attributed revenue, and the resulting payout for each period.

5. Content Standards & Compliance

Creator confirms they own, or are fully authorized to license, all rights to the Work, and that it does not infringe any third party's copyright, trademark, or other rights. The Work must comply with AniLoka's Community Guidelines and Copyright Policy. AniLoka may remove content that violates these policies or is the subject of a valid takedown notice, and will notify Creator when this happens.

6. Term & Termination

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for [initial term, e.g. 12 months], renewing automatically unless either party gives [e.g. 30 days] written notice. Either party may terminate earlier for material breach that isn't fixed within [e.g. 14 days] of written notice. On termination, AniLoka will pay out any earnings already owed on the normal payout schedule, and the exclusivity obligation in Section 2 ends.

7. Representations & Warranties

Creator represents that they have the legal right and authority to enter this Agreement and to grant the rights described in Section 1, and that publishing the Work will not violate any other agreement Creator is bound by.

8. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep the specific commercial terms of this Agreement (revenue figures, payout details, tier criteria) confidential, except where disclosure is required by law or to each party's professional advisors.

9. Limitation of Liability

To the extent permitted by law, neither party is liable to the other for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from this Agreement. Nothing in this section limits either party's liability for fraud, willful misconduct, or obligations that cannot be limited by law.

10. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of [India / your jurisdiction]. Any dispute not resolved informally within [e.g. 30 days] will be referred to [arbitration / courts of a specified city].

11. General

This Agreement (with any signed Schedules/addenda) is the entire agreement between the parties regarding the Work, and supersedes prior discussions on the same subject. It may only be amended in writing, signed by both parties. If any provision is found unenforceable, the rest of the Agreement remains in effect. Neither party may assign this Agreement without the other's written consent.
