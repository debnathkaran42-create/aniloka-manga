About Us

Last updated: July 2026

India's Own Manga Streaming Platform

Japan has Manga Plus. South Korea has Webtoon. AniLoka exists to give India the same thing — a home-grown platform built specifically for Indian manga readers, with the reading experience, pricing, and payment methods (UPI!) that actually make sense here.

What We're Building

  • A clean, ad-supported free reading experience with an optional Premium tier.
  • Direct UPI payments — no need for international cards.
  • A real path for manga creators to publish and earn from their work.
  • Anime streaming, planned as a future addition.
