Copyright Policy

Last updated: July 2026

AniLoka respects the intellectual property rights of manga creators, publishers, and rightsholders, and expects everyone who uses or publishes on the platform to do the same.

1. Our Position

AniLoka is built to give creators a legitimate way to publish and monetize their work — not to host unauthorized scans or scanlations of copyrighted manga without permission. Only content that is original, properly licensed, or otherwise authorized for publication may be uploaded.

2. Creator Responsibility

By uploading manga to AniLoka, a creator or publisher confirms that:

  • They own the content, or hold a valid license/permission to publish it.
  • Publishing it on AniLoka doesn't infringe on any third party's copyright, trademark, or other rights.

3. Reporting Infringement

If you believe content on AniLoka infringes your copyright, please follow the process in our Copyright Takedown Policy.

4. Consequences of Infringement

Content found to be infringing will be removed. Accounts responsible for repeated infringement will be suspended or permanently terminated, in line with our repeat-infringer policy.
