Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy explains how AniLoka uses cookies and similar local storage technologies.

1. What We Use

  • Session cookie — keeps you logged in (used when a real backend is connected).
  • CSRF token cookie — a security measure that protects your account from cross-site request forgery attacks.
  • Local storage — in local-only mode (no backend connected), your account, library, and reading progress are stored in your browser's local storage instead of cookies.

2. Third-Party Cookies

If Google AdSense is enabled, Google may set its own cookies to serve relevant ads. AniLoka does not control these — see Google's Privacy Policy for details, and their Ads Settings page to opt out of personalized ads.

3. Managing Cookies

You can clear cookies and local storage anytime through your browser settings. Doing so will log you out and, in local-only mode, will erase your local library and reading history.

4. No Cross-Site Tracking

Beyond what our ad partner does on its own, AniLoka does not track you across other websites.
